Email template
Header: Includes sender's logo, company name, and contact information.
Subject Line: A concise summary of the email's content.
Greeting: Salutation addressing the recipient.
Body Content: Main message or information.
Call to Action (CTA): Prompts recipients to take a specific action.
Footer: Legal disclaimers, unsubscribe links, and contact details.
Personalization Variables: Placeholders for dynamic personalization, such as recipient's name.
